# Domain Discovery

## Purpose

Domain Discovery identifies and documents the problem space, business context, and strategic intent that guide all SkillForge architecture and design decisions.

This phase ensures the entire team shares:
- Clear understanding of what business problem SkillForge solves
- The organizational domains and subdomains involved
- The distinction between core, supporting, and generic domains
- The constraints, opportunities, and strategic priorities

## Outputs

1. **01-BUSINESS_DOMAIN.md** — The business problem, market opportunity, and strategic context
2. **02-CORE_DOMAINS.md** — The primary operational areas where SkillForge creates value

## Principles

- **Problem-First**: We understand the problem before designing solutions
- **Stakeholder Alignment**: Business, product, and technical stakeholders use shared language
- **Domain Clarity**: Clear boundaries between domains prevent feature creep and architectural confusion
- **Evolutionary**: Domain understanding evolves as we learn; documentation is living, not static

## How to Use These Documents

1. Read to align on business intent before any architecture or implementation
2. Reference when making strategic decisions about scope or priorities
3. Update as market conditions or strategic priorities shift
4. Share with stakeholders to ensure alignment
