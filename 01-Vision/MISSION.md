# SkillForge Mission

## Purpose Statement

**SkillForge helps learners acquire, demonstrate, and verify real-world skills through competency-based learning, enabling career growth and employment.**

---

## The Mission in Practice

### For Learners

We enable learners to:

1. **Discover** what skills are valuable in real job markets
2. **Learn** through engaging, project-based missions
3. **Apply** skills in authentic, real-world projects
4. **Demonstrate** competency through evidence
5. **Verify** competency through official credentials
6. **Grow** continuously with AI-guided recommendations
7. **Own** a portable, lifetime professional profile (Skill Passport)
8. **Access** opportunity based on demonstrated ability, not background

**Promise**: You will develop real, verifiable skills and have the credentials to prove it.

---

### For Instructors

We enable instructors to:

1. **Design** learning missions and competency frameworks
2. **Reach** more learners without geographic constraints
3. **Assess** competency through evidence and rubrics
4. **Mentor** learners with AI-assisted feedback
5. **Build** reputation as subject matter experts
6. **Measure** learning outcomes and impact
7. **Iterate** based on learner data and feedback
8. **Collaborate** with other instructors and domain experts

**Promise**: You will create high-impact learning experiences at scale.

---

### For Organizations (Future)

We enable organizations to:

1. **Develop** workforce competencies systematically
2. **Customize** learning paths for business needs
3. **Measure** ROI on learning investments
4. **Reduce** time-to-productivity for new employees
5. **Enable** career development and internal mobility
6. **Access** verified talent for hiring

**Promise**: You will build a skilled, adaptable workforce aligned to business strategy.

---

### For Employers (Future)

We enable employers to:

1. **Access** pre-vetted talent pools filtered by demonstrated competency
2. **Trust** credentials because they're evidence-backed
3. **Reduce** recruiting cost and time-to-hire
4. **Verify** candidate claims efficiently
5. **Track** employee growth and capability development
6. **Contribute** to competency frameworks and market signals

**Promise**: You will hire faster, with higher-quality signal, at lower cost.

## Core Values

### 1. Learner-Centric

Every decision prioritizes learner outcomes and learner agency. Learners own their data, their goals, and their credentials.

### 2. Evidence-Based

We never compromise on evidence quality. Competency is proven through demonstrated capability, not assumptions or proxies.

### 3. Inclusive

SkillForge is designed for all learners:
- Multiple languages (English + Hausa first)
- Accessible to people with disabilities
- Works on any device, any connection speed
- Affordable or free for emerging market contexts
- Transcends geographic, institutional, and socioeconomic boundaries

### 4. Quality First

We prioritize quality over quantity. One excellent learning mission is worth more than ten mediocre ones. We scale quality, not mediocrity.

### 5. Transparent

We communicate clearly about:
- How AI makes decisions about learner opportunity
- What evidence supports credentials
- How competencies are defined
- How assessments are evaluated
- What data we collect and how we use it

### 6. Collaborative

We partner with educators, employers, and learners to continuously improve. SkillForge is built with communities, not for them.

### 7. Practical

Every competency has real-world application. Every skill is taught through projects. Every credential predicts job performance.

### 8. Equitable

We actively work to reduce barriers to opportunity. Pricing, design, and feature development consider the constraints of emerging market contexts.

## What We Don't Do

### We are NOT an LMS

- We don't organize around courses
- We don't manage institutional seat time
- We don't issue degrees
- We're not designed for compliance-focused training

### We are NOT a Bootcamp

- We don't require cohorts or synchronous participation
- We don't have fixed start dates or completion times
- We're not instructor-intensive or high-cost
- We support both depth and breadth (not specialization only)

### We are NOT a Certification Body

- We don't gate credentials behind expensive exams
- We don't define competencies in isolation from practice
- We're not geographically limited
- Credential value comes from evidence, not monopoly

### We are NOT a Job Board

- (Yet). We're learning-first and employment outcomes second
- We don't place learners; we prepare them
- Hiring is not our primary function

## Strategy

### Three Strategic Pillars

#### 1. Competency Clarity

**Goal**: Make skills and competencies transparent and actionable

- Define competency frameworks for in-demand roles
- Show learners clear paths from current state to target competency
- Provide employers with skill taxonomy they can trust
- Continuously update as markets evolve

#### 2. Evidence Collection

**Goal**: Build portfolio of real, employer-verifiable artifacts

- Structure learning around real-world projects
- Create assessment rubrics tied to competencies
- Collect continuous evidence (not episodic testing)
- Make evidence portable and cryptographically verified

#### 3. AI-Powered Personalization

**Goal**: Deliver individualized learning experiences at scale

- Build learner profiles from behavior and preference data
- Recommend missions and projects based on goals and learning style
- Adapt difficulty and pacing in real-time
- Provide predictive insights and interventions
- Make all recommendations transparent and appealable

- **Employment**: 50%+ of learners in relevant roles within 12 months
- **Satisfaction**: 4.5+/5 NPS; 85%+ would recommend SkillForge

- **Equity**: 50%+ of learners from underrepresented groups; 40%+ content in Hausa

### Employer Trust Metrics

- **Hiring**: 2K+ hires annually using SkillForge credentials
- **Retention**: 85%+ of SkillForge hires retained after 12 months
- **Quality**: Hiring managers rate SkillForge candidates 4.5+/5 for job readiness
- **Cost**: Recruiting cost-per-hire reduced by 50% vs. traditional recruiting
- **Verification**: 100% of credentials can be employer-verified in <5 minutes

### Educational Quality Metrics

- **Completion**: 70%+ of learners complete assigned learning paths
- **Mastery**: 85%+ of learners demonstrate competency before credential issuance
- **Evidence Quality**: 90%+ of evidence artifacts meet instructor quality standards
- **Feedback Quality**: 95%+ of learners find feedback helpful and actionable
- **Adaptation**: 80%+ of recommendations are taken up by learners

- **Year 1**: 50K active learners; 100+ learning missions; $0-1M ARR
- **Year 3**: 500K active learners; 1K+ missions; $10M ARR
